Key Rules And Formulas

  • Angles on a line = 180
  • Angles around a point = 360
  • Sum in triangle = 180
  • Interior sum of polygon = (n - 2) * 180
  • Exterior sum of polygon = 360

Worked Examples

Find angle on a line

  1. Subtract known angle from 180

Answer: 116

Interior sum of octagon

  1. Use (n - 2)*180 with n=8

Answer: 1080

Regular polygon with exterior 24

  1. Sides = 360 / 24

Answer: 15 sides
